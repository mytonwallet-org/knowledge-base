# Access Troubleshooting

## Forgot Password

If you forgot your password but have your **recovery phrase**:
1. Uninstall and reinstall the app (or clear app data).
2. Import your wallet using the recovery phrase.
3. Set a new password.

If you forgot your password and **do not have your recovery phrase**:
- There is no way to recover access. MyTonWallet cannot reset your password.
- Your funds remain on the blockchain but are inaccessible without the phrase.

## Forgot PIN

Same as forgetting a password — re-import with your recovery phrase to set a new PIN.

## Biometric Unlock Stopped Working

- Check that biometrics are still enabled in Settings → Security.
- Your device may have updated or reset biometric data. Re-enable biometric unlock in the app.
- On some devices, biometric access expires after a system update. Enter your password once, then re-enable biometrics.

## App Is Locked and Won't Accept Password

- Make sure you're entering the correct password (watch for caps lock, keyboard language).
- If you have multiple accounts, make sure you're entering the password for the active account.
- As a last resort, reinstall the app and import with your recovery phrase.

## Lost Recovery Phrase

If you still have access to the app:
- **Immediately** go to Settings → Security → Back Up Secret Words and write it down.
- You can also export your private key as an additional backup.

If you've lost both the recovery phrase and access to the app:
- Funds are permanently lost. There is no recovery.

## Device Lost or Stolen

If someone has your device but not your password:
- Your wallet is protected by password/PIN/biometrics. They cannot access it without authenticating.
- For extra safety, import your wallet on another device using your recovery phrase and transfer funds to a new wallet (with a new recovery phrase).

If someone has both your device and your password:
- They can access your funds. Transfer everything to a new wallet immediately from another device.
