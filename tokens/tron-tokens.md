# TRON Tokens

## TRX (TRON)

The native token of the TRON blockchain. Used to:
- Pay network fees
- Provide energy and bandwidth for transactions

## TRC-20 Tokens

TRC-20 is the token standard on TRON. The most common:
- **USDT** — Tether stablecoin (TRON is one of the most popular chains for USDT transfers)

## Energy and Bandwidth

TRON uses a unique fee model:
- **Bandwidth** is consumed by all transactions (a small amount, replenishes over time).
- **Energy** is consumed by smart contract interactions (like TRC-20 transfers).

If you don't have enough energy, the transaction fee is paid in TRX. Simple TRX transfers are very cheap. TRC-20 token transfers (like USDT) cost more because they use energy.

## USDT on TRON

TRON is one of the most widely used networks for USDT transfers due to its relatively low fees. When sending USDT on TRON, make sure:
- The recipient address is a TRON address (starts with `T`).
- You have some TRX for the network fee.
