# Importing and Managing Tokens

## Importing a Token

If a token doesn't appear in your wallet automatically:

1. Go to **Settings → Tokens → Import Token**.
2. Paste the token's **contract address** (you can usually find this on the token's website or a blockchain explorer).
3. The app looks up the token details (name, symbol, decimals).
4. Confirm to add it to your token list.

Token import is available for TON (Jettons) and Solana (SPL tokens) where the chain SDK supports it.

## Pinning Tokens

Pin your most-used tokens to keep them at the top of your token list:
- On the Home tab, long-press a token and choose **Pin**, or manage pinned tokens in Settings → Tokens.

**Pinned Tokens** keep important assets at the top of the list. Staking positions pin automatically, and you can unpin them if you prefer.

## Quick Token Actions

The Assets list also provides a shortcut menu for tokens. Depending on the token, this can include:

- Top up
- Send
- Swap
- Stake or earn
- Hide

## Hiding Tokens

To remove a token from your list without affecting your balance:
- Long-press the token and choose **Hide**, or go to Settings → Tokens → Hidden Tokens.
- Hidden tokens still exist in your wallet — you can unhide them anytime.

Useful for hiding spam tokens, airdrops you don't want, or tokens with tiny balances.

## Always-Shown Tokens

Some tokens are marked as "always shown" — they appear in your list even with zero balance. You can manage this in Settings → Tokens.

## Tiny Transfers

Very small token balances (dust) can clutter your list. Toggle **Show Tiny Transfers** in Settings → Tokens to hide or show them.
