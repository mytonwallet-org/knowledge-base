# Tokens Overview

## What Are Tokens?

Tokens are digital assets that live on a blockchain. There are two kinds:

### Native Tokens
Each blockchain has one native token used to pay network fees:
- **TON** on the TON blockchain
- **TRX** on the TRON blockchain
- **SOL** on the Solana blockchain

You always need a small amount of the native token to pay fees, even when transacting with other tokens.

### Custom Tokens
Additional tokens created on top of a blockchain:
- **Jettons** on TON (e.g., USDT, NOT, DOGS)
- **TRC-20 tokens** on TRON (e.g., USDT)
- **SPL tokens** on Solana (e.g., USDT, USDC)

The most common custom token is **USDT** (Tether), a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar, available on all three chains.

## Your Token List

The Home tab displays all tokens you hold with a non-zero balance. For each token you see:
- **Symbol and name**
- **Balance** (how much you hold)
- **Value** in your chosen display currency
- **24h price change** (percentage)

Tap a token to see its **price chart** and **transaction history**.

## Display Settings

- **Base currency** — choose which fiat currency to display values in (USD, EUR, RUB, etc.) via Settings.
- **Pin tokens** — keep your most-used tokens at the top of the list.
- **Hide tokens** — remove tokens you don't want to see.
- **Show tiny transfers** — toggle whether very small balances are visible (Settings → Tokens).

See `tokens/import-hide.md` for details on managing your token list.
