# TON Tokens

## TON (Toncoin)

The native token of the TON blockchain. Used to:
- Pay network fees for all TON transactions
- Stake for rewards
- Pay for TON DNS domain registration and renewal
- Interact with TON dApps

## Jettons

Jettons are custom tokens on the TON blockchain (similar to ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum). Common Jettons include:
- **USDT** — Tether stablecoin (pegged to USD)
- **NOT** — Notcoin
- **DOGS** — Dogs token

When sending Jettons, you pay the network fee in TON. Make sure you always keep a small TON balance for fees.

## Liquid Staking Tokens

When you stake TON through liquid staking, you receive a token representing your staked position:
- **tsTON**, **stTON**, or similar depending on the pool

These tokens increase in value over time as staking rewards accumulate. You can hold them, transfer them, or use them in DeFi, and redeem them for TON + rewards when you unstake.

## USDT on TON

USDT (Tether) on TON is one of the most popular tokens. It's the same stablecoin as USDT on other chains, but using TON's fast and cheap transactions. When sending USDT on TON, the recipient must also be on the TON network — you can't send TON USDT directly to a TRON or Solana address (use cross-chain swap for that).
