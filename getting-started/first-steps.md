# First Steps After Setup

## Navigating the App

### Mobile (iOS / Android)
The app has three main tabs at the bottom:
- **Home** — your balances, tokens, and recent activity. This is where you send, receive, swap, stake, and buy crypto.
- **Explore** — browse and connect to decentralized apps (dApps). Tap a dApp to open it in the in-app browser.
- **Settings** — account management, security, appearance, and preferences. Long-press the Settings tab to quickly switch between accounts.

### Web / Desktop / Extension
Similar layout, but navigation is at the top or side depending on screen size. dApps open in your regular browser rather than an in-app browser.

### Telegram Mini App
Opens inside Telegram with the same core features. Some platform-specific features (Ledger, deep links) are not available.

## What to Do First

1. **Back up your recovery phrase** if you haven't already — Settings → Security → Back Up Secret Words.
2. **Fund your wallet** — tap Receive to get your address and share it with someone sending you crypto, or tap Buy to purchase with a card.
3. **Explore your tokens** — the Home tab shows your balances. Tap any token to see its price chart and transaction history.
4. **Customize** — choose your theme (light/dark), language, and accent color in Settings.

## Key Actions from the Home Screen

- **Send** — transfer tokens to an address or TON DNS domain
- **Receive** — show your address and QR code
- **Swap** — exchange one token for another
- **Stake** — earn rewards by staking TON (visible when you hold TON)
- **Buy** — purchase crypto with a bank card via Moonpay

## Tips

- You can hold multiple accounts in one app. Add more from Settings.
- Each account has separate addresses for TON, TRON, and Solana — make sure you share the right one for the chain someone is sending on.
- Pin your most-used tokens to the top of the list for quick access.
- Enable push notifications (Settings → Notifications) to get alerted about incoming transfers.
