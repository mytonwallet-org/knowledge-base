# Buying & Selling Crypto

## Buying Crypto

You can buy crypto with a bank card directly in the app.

1. Tap **Buy**
2. Choose the token and amount
3. Complete the provider's payment and verification flow
4. Receive the purchased asset in your wallet

Some buy flows route between **MoonPay** and **Avanchange**, depending on the asset, region, or selected payment currency.

## Selling Crypto

MyTonWallet also supports a native off-ramp flow for selling supported crypto to a bank card in supported countries.

Typical flow:

1. Open **Sell** or the off-ramp option
2. Choose the token and amount
3. Complete the provider's identity and payout steps
4. Receive fiat through the supported payout method

## Availability

Card purchase and sell availability depends on:

- Your region
- The selected asset
- The provider currently available in your app version

If **Buy** or **Sell** is missing, the feature may not be available for your country or current account context.

## Fees And Verification

- Providers charge their own service fees
- Blockchain fees may still apply when moving funds on-chain
- First-time users may need identity verification
- Processing time depends on provider review and banking rails

## Important Notes

- These services are provided by third parties, not by MyTonWallet itself
- Provider options and supported countries can change
- Always review the final rate, payout amount, and fees before confirming
