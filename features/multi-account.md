# Multiple Accounts

## Why Multiple Accounts?

You can keep separate wallets for:

- Personal and business funds
- Different strategies or blockchains
- Hardware-wallet accounts
- View-only or watched wallets

## Creating Another Account

1. Open the account switcher, wallet gallery, or **Add Account**
2. Choose **Create New**, **Import**, **Ledger**, or **Watch Account**
3. Complete setup

## Switching Accounts

### Mobile

- Long-press the **Settings** tab
- Or open the account switcher from the wallet header
- Swipe gestures can also help switch wallets

### Web, Desktop, And Extension

- Click your account name or avatar
- Desktop contexts also support right-click shortcuts

## Wallet Gallery

The **Wallet Gallery** makes account management easier. It supports:

- Tile or list view
- Reordering
- Renaming
- Deleting from the app
- Filtering Ledger or view-only wallets
- Showing balance previews and total-balance summaries
- Showing address endings or multichain badges for easier identification

## View-Only Wallets

Watch accounts and explorer-style wallets can appear alongside regular wallets, but they stay read-only. See `features/wallet-explorer.md`.

## Shared Settings Vs Wallet Data

Each account has its own:

- Addresses
- Balances
- Activity
- NFTs
- Connected dApps
- Staking positions

Global settings such as theme, language, and lock behavior are usually shared across the app.

## Account Limits

- Push notifications are limited to up to `3` wallets
- There is no practical need to keep every watched or active wallet in one list, so many users use Wallet Gallery filters and watch mode to stay organized
