# TON Proxy

## What Is TON Proxy?

TON Proxy routes your connection through the TON network, providing an additional layer of privacy when browsing TON-based sites and services.

## How to Enable

Go to **Settings** and toggle **TON Proxy** on.

When enabled, requests to TON sites are routed through the TON proxy network instead of connecting directly.

## When to Use It

- When browsing TON-based websites and dApps for extra privacy
- When you want to access TON network services that may not be directly reachable

## Notes

- TON Proxy affects connections to TON sites only — regular internet browsing is not affected.
- Enabling it may slightly increase latency for TON site requests due to the additional routing.
