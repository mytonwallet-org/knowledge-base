# Staking

Staking lets you earn rewards by locking your tokens to help secure the network.

## TON Staking

### How to Stake TON

1. From the Home tab, access the **Staking** section (visible when you hold TON).
2. Enter the amount of TON to stake.
3. Review the estimated APY and confirm the transaction.

The app automatically selects the best staking pool for you. Rewards accumulate automatically and are visible in your staking balance.

### How It Works

Your staked TON goes into a **liquid staking** pool. You receive a **liquid token** (e.g., tsTON) representing your staked position. This token increases in value over time as staking rewards accumulate. You can hold, transfer, or use it in DeFi — your funds aren't truly locked. When you unstake, you exchange the liquid token back for TON plus accumulated rewards.

## Ethena USDe Staking

If you hold **USDe** (Ethena's stablecoin on TON), you can stake it to earn yield. Your deposited USDe is used in Ethena's trading and investment strategies to generate rewards automatically and transparently.

### How to Stake USDe

1. From the Home tab, access the **Earn** section.
2. Select the **Ethena USDe** staking option.
3. Enter the amount of USDe to stake (minimum **1 USDe**).
4. Review the estimated APY and confirm the transaction.

When you stake USDe, you receive **tsUSDe** — a liquid token representing your staked position. Rewards accumulate in the value of tsUSDe over time.

Withdrawals follow Ethena's standard withdrawal period: unstaked funds are returned to your account **within 7 days**.

Ethena staking is fully decentralized, operated by Ethena's official audited smart contracts.

Ethena and USDe are independent of MyTonWallet. Treat USDe staking as a third-party product surfaced inside the wallet, not as a native MyTonWallet guarantee.

### Unstaking USDe

1. Go to your Ethena staking position.
2. Tap **Unstake** and enter the amount.
3. Confirm the transaction.

Unstaked funds enter a **7-day withdrawal period**. During this time, the amount is locked and shown as a pending unstake request. Once the 7 days pass, tap **Claim** to receive your USDe back.

## MY Token Staking

**MY** (MyTonWallet Coin) is the wallet's own token on the TON blockchain. If you hold MY tokens, you can stake them to earn rewards.

MY staking works through a dedicated staking pool. You deposit your MY tokens, and they earn rewards over time. The staking and unstaking process is similar to TON staking — stake from the Staking section, claim rewards, and unstake when ready (subject to a cooldown period with commission rates that vary by unstaking timing).

## Claiming Rewards

- **TON staking**: rewards are reflected in the increasing value of your liquid token. No manual claiming needed.
- **Ethena USDe staking**: rewards accumulate in tsUSDe value. No manual claiming needed.
- **MY staking**: rewards may need to be claimed manually from the staking screen.

## Unstaking

1. Go to your staking position.
2. Tap **Unstake** and enter the amount (or unstake all).
3. Confirm the transaction.

There may be a **cooldown period** before unstaked TON returns to your available balance. The app shows the estimated wait time.

## Risks

- Staking is generally low-risk, but rewards are not guaranteed.
- Liquid staking tokens may trade at a slight discount or premium to their underlying value.
- Your staked tokens are locked for the unstaking cooldown period — you can't send them during that time (unless using liquid staking).
- MY staking unstaking commission varies depending on how early you unstake.
