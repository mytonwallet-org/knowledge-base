# Activity Feed & History

## What It Shows

The Activity feed is the wallet's history view for transfers, swaps, staking operations, NFT actions, and other blockchain events related to your wallet.

## Main Features

The Activity feed includes:

- Grouping technical or change transactions so history is easier to read
- Token icons for swaps and other token actions
- Better recognition of transaction types
- Fiat-equivalent amounts in your selected base currency
- Faster display of incoming transfers, especially on TON
- Names for saved contacts and some known addresses in history

## Balance Hiding

Activity does not have a separate balance hiding control. Balance hiding is managed from the Home screen.

## Transfer Links

After sending, the app can generate a shareable transfer link immediately, even before the blockchain confirmation is fully final. This is useful when you need to show that a payment has already been initiated.

## Base Currency

Fiat values in Activity depend on your selected **base currency**. You can change it from:

- The wallet card
- `Settings → Assets & Activity → Base Currency`

## View-Only And Explorer History

When you add watched wallets or open external wallets in view mode, their public activity is also visible without importing private keys.

## Important Notes

- Blockchain history can include internal or technical operations that are not meaningful payments
- dApp previews and labels improve readability, but they are not a guarantee of final on-chain outcome
- If you need help with a pending or failed transfer, see `troubleshooting/transactions.md`
