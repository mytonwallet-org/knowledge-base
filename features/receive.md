# Receiving Crypto

## How To Receive

1. From the Home tab, tap **Receive**.
2. Choose the chain or asset you want to receive.
3. Share your address, QR code, or payment link with the sender.

Your TON, TRON, and Solana addresses are different. Always share the address for the correct chain.

## Sharing Options

- **Copy address**
- **QR code**
- **Share**
- **Invoice or payment link** with a pre-filled amount
- **Deposit link** for a selected token

## Invoices

You can create an invoice with a specific amount:

1. Open **Receive**
2. Enter an amount or use **Create Invoice**
3. Share the generated link

When the sender opens the link, their wallet can pre-fill the payment details.

## Token-Specific Deposit Links

Deposit links can also support a chosen TON token, not only the native address. This is useful when you want to request a specific asset instead of asking the sender to choose it manually.

## Wallet Sharing

MyTonWallet also uses cleaner wallet-sharing links with `my.tt`, especially for view-only or explorer-style sharing. See `features/wallet-explorer.md`.

## Important Notes

- **Always verify the chain**: funds sent on the wrong network may be lost
- **Incoming transactions may appear quickly** in Activity, especially on TON, but blockchain finality can still take longer
- **Tiny balances may be hidden** unless you enable `Show Tiny Transfers`
- If you want chat-based payment requests inside Telegram, see `features/push.md`
