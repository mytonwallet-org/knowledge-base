# Vesting

## What Is Vesting?

Vesting is a schedule that locks tokens and releases them gradually over time. If you received tokens with a vesting schedule (e.g., from a team allocation, airdrop, or investment), they become available ("vest") according to a predefined timeline.

## Viewing Your Vesting

If you have vesting positions, they appear in the Home tab or account details. You can see:
- Total vested amount
- Amount already unlocked
- Amount still locked
- Upcoming unlock dates

## Unfreezing Tokens

When tokens reach their unlock date, you can **unfreeze** them:
1. Go to your vesting position.
2. Tap **Unfreeze** (or **Claim**) for the available amount.
3. Confirm the transaction.

Unfrozen tokens move to your regular balance and can be used normally.

## Notes

- Vesting schedules are set by the token issuer and cannot be changed.
- You may have multiple vesting positions from different sources.
- Unfreezing requires a small network fee.
