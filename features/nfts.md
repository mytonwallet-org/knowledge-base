# NFTs & Collectibles

## Your NFT Gallery

NFTs and collectibles appear in the **Assets** area of the wallet, usually grouped by collection.

Supported collectible types include:

- Regular TON and Solana NFTs
- **Telegram Gifts**
- **TON DNS domains**
- **MyTonWallet Cards**

The wallet includes pinned collection tabs and improved separation of Telegram Gifts from regular NFTs.

## Viewing NFTs

When you open an NFT, you can see:

- Full image or media
- Collection information
- Ownership details
- Marketplace links
- Individual attributes such as traits, models, or textures

The wallet also includes an expanded NFT attributes viewer.

## Sending NFTs

1. Open the NFT
2. Tap **Send**
3. Enter the destination
4. Confirm

Bulk transfer is also available for supported NFT collections.

## Sending NFTs Through `@push`

You can also send TON NFTs and on-chain Telegram Gifts through `@push` inside Telegram chats. See `features/push.md`.

## Telegram Gifts

Telegram Gifts are handled as on-chain collectibles in the wallet. Features include:

- Improved display so Gifts are easier to distinguish from other NFTs
- Pinned Telegram Gifts
- Sending gifts through `@push`

## MyTonWallet Cards

MyTonWallet Cards are special collectibles used for wallet personalization. They can unlock:

- Custom wallet-card backgrounds
- Color palettes
- Appearance options in `Settings → Appearance`

## Hiding Or Burning NFTs

- You can hide unwanted NFTs or collections to reduce clutter
- Spam NFTs may also be burnable, depending on the asset

Always verify what you are hiding or burning, because burning is irreversible.

## TON DNS Domains As NFTs

TON DNS domains are NFTs on TON. They can be viewed, transferred, linked, and renewed. See `features/ton-dns.md`.
