# Backing Up Your Wallet

## Why Back Up?

Your recovery phrase is the **only way** to restore your wallet if you lose your device, uninstall the app, or forget your password. Without it, your funds are permanently inaccessible. MyTonWallet is non-custodial — we cannot recover your wallet for you.

## Viewing Your Recovery Phrase

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Back Up Secret Words**.
2. Confirm your identity (password, PIN, or biometric).
3. Your 24 words are displayed in order.
4. Write them down on paper. Do not screenshot, copy to notes apps, or store digitally.

## Verifying Your Backup

After displaying the phrase, the app asks you to verify a few randomly selected words. This ensures you wrote them down correctly.

## Exporting Your Private Key

For advanced users, you can also export your private key:
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Back Up Private Key**.
2. Confirm your identity.
3. The private key is displayed.

The private key is chain-specific and gives access only to that chain's funds. The recovery phrase gives access to all chains.

## Storage Recommendations

- Write on paper, not digitally. Digital copies can be hacked.
- Store in a secure physical location (safe, lockbox).
- Consider keeping copies in multiple locations in case of fire, flood, etc.
- Never share your recovery phrase or private key with anyone.
- Never enter it on any website or form. MyTonWallet will never ask for it except during wallet import.

## What Happens If You Lose Your Backup?

If you lose your recovery phrase and still have access to the app:
- Immediately go to Settings → Security → Back Up Secret Words and write it down again.

If you lose both the phrase and access to the app:
- Your funds are permanently lost. There is no recovery mechanism.
